The Main Goal

Design a visual identity of the brand, which is elegant and stylish. (Logo design, business card, social media posts, and website).

Execution

1. I designed a classy, elegant, bold, and simple logotyp to show the brand character. I decided to use the brown, pastel, neutral color to make it calm in order to add the logo on social media pictures, and to be able to fit any nails' color and design.


2. Logo was designed for an online and print use, in both large format for physical wall painting and compact size of the business card.

Further Steps
